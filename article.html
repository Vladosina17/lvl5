What about home plants?

Indoor plants - plants that are grown in rooms and in public spaces. Most houseplants come from the tropics and subtropics. Since residential or public premises are characterized by conditions not so favorable for plants as when growing them in greenhouses (for example, the lateral direction of light, its small amount, dry air, plenty of dust, drafts, uneven temperature), to achieve a healthy state and proper development Plants use special tricks.
Conditions in public and office premises are usually even less favorable than in residential, so the most unpretentious indoor plants are grown here.
